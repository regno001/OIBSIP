Ratan Tata

1937 – 2024

"I don’t believe in taking the right decisions. I take decisions and then make them right."

Biography

Ratan Naval Tata, born on December 28, 1937, was one of India’s most visionary industrialists and philanthropists. Under his leadership, the Tata Group transformed into a global powerhouse, acquiring brands like Jaguar Land Rover, Corus Steel, and Tetley Tea. Beyond business, he championed ethical leadership, innovation, and social welfare through the Tata Trusts, impacting millions of lives in education, healthcare, and rural development.

Even with his immense success, he remained humble, respected, and deeply loved by the nation. His passing on October 9, 2024, marks the end of an era, but his legacy of integrity, compassion, and progress will continue to inspire generations.
